About Us

Coffee and Bagels is a dating website created for students to meet new people and build connections. Students can create their user profiles and depending on their responses, they will be matched with others. For this website, we've created a compatability algorithm which calculates how compatible a user is to others.

How does it work?

When signing up, a user provides information to create their profile. This would include details such as email, name, username, password, etc. Users would also provide answers to a standard questionnaire; this questionnaire and the users responses are used in evaluating the compatibility of potential matches that the user may get along with.
